# Credex: User Interviews & Discovery

During the validation phase, we spoke with 3 target customers to understand their pain points regarding SaaS and AI spend.

## Interview 1: Sarah T. (Fractional CFO, Series A SaaS)
- **Current Stack Size**: ~40 employees.
- **Pain Point**: "Every engineer just uses their Brex card to sign up for whatever AI tool is trending on Twitter that week. I saw three different Cursor subscriptions, 10 GitHub Copilot charges, and someone expensing Claude Pro. I have no idea what these tools do or if they overlap."
- **Attempted Solutions**: She tried to audit it via a spreadsheet, but it took 4 hours and was outdated immediately.
- **Credex Reaction**: Extremely positive. "If you could just plug into Brex, show me the overlap, and give me a button to cancel the redundant ones, I'd pay 20% of whatever you save me."

## Interview 2: Marcus R. (VP Engineering, DevTools Startup)
- **Current Stack Size**: 65 engineers.
- **Pain Point**: "We buy Copilot for everyone automatically during onboarding. But half the team has switched to Cursor and is expensing that too. We're paying Microsoft twice for the exact same autocomplete functionality."
- **Attempted Solutions**: Sent out a Google Form asking developers which tool they prefer. Only 40% responded.
- **Credex Reaction**: Positive, but raised a concern: "Don't cancel things automatically. I need to approve it, otherwise developers will revolt if their favorite tool stops working mid-sprint."

## Interview 3: Elena M. (Founder/CEO, Seed Stage AI App)
- **Current Stack Size**: 12 employees.
- **Pain Point**: "I don't have time to negotiate with OpenAI for an enterprise tier. We just put 12 ChatGPT Plus accounts on my personal Amex. It's a mess for accounting, but it's the fastest way."
- **Attempted Solutions**: Reached out to OpenAI sales; never heard back because they are too small.
- **Credex Reaction**: Loved the "Syndicate/Wholesale" idea. "If you can pool me with other startups to get me the enterprise admin dashboard and a 10% discount, I'll sign up today."
