# Credex Key Performance Indicators (Metrics)

To measure the success of the Credex AI Spend Audit platform post-launch, we will track the following core metrics.

## 1. Top of Funnel (Acquisition)
- **Audit Completion Rate**: `(Completed Audits / Landing Page Views) * 100`
  - *Target*: > 15%
  - *Why*: This measures the effectiveness of our hero copy and the friction of the `AuditForm.tsx`.
- **K-Factor (Viral Coefficient)**: Average number of new users generated by a single existing user sharing their OpenGraph link.
  - *Target*: > 0.2
  - *Why*: Indicates the shareability and "wow factor" of the savings numbers.

## 2. Mid Funnel (Activation)
- **Lead Capture Conversion Rate**: `(Emails Captured / Completed Audits) * 100`
  - *Target*: > 40%
  - *Why*: Measures the strength of the "Playbook" lead magnet. Since they have already invested time taking the audit, this should be high.
- **Identified Savings per Audit**: The average `$totalAnnualSavings` found per user.
  - *Why*: If this number is too low, our value proposition collapses. If it's consistently high, we have a strong business case for Tier 1 monetization.

## 3. Bottom of Funnel (Monetization & Retention)
- **Playbook Implementation Rate**: Percentage of users who take action (downgrade/consolidate) within 14 days of receiving their email.
  - *Why*: If users don't act on the playbook, they won't pay us to do it for them.
- **Conversion to Syndicate**: Percentage of audited users who sign up for the $99/mo wholesale reseller tier.
  - *Target*: 3-5% of qualified leads.
